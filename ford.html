Ford

Built Ford Tough. Engineered to Perform.

Ford has been a staple in American performance and innovation for over a century. From the racetrack to the drag strip, Ford delivers power, reliability, and style in every model.

Featured Vehicles

Ford Mustang GT

America’s Muscle Icon
The Ford Mustang GT continues to set the standard for modern muscle. With its aggressive stance, naturally aspirated V8 engine, and roaring exhaust note, it captures the heart of performance enthusiasts worldwide.

Ford Mustang Shelby GT500

The Most Powerful Mustang Ever
The Shelby GT500 is the pinnacle of Ford performance. Featuring a supercharged 5.2L V8 producing over 700 horsepower, track-ready suspension, and aggressive aerodynamics, it delivers brutal acceleration and refined control in one iconic package.

Ford GT

Race-Bred Supercar
Inspired by Ford's Le Mans heritage, the Ford GT is a carbon-fiber masterpiece. With a twin-turbo V6, active aerodynamics, and striking design, it is built for speed, precision, and dominance on both track and street.

Ford Focus RS

Hot Hatch Legend
The Focus RS blends rally-inspired handling with turbocharged power in a practical hatchback form. With all-wheel drive, launch control, and performance-tuned suspension, it’s as fun on the track as it is on city streets.
