About VeloCiti Motors

At VeloCiti Motors, we don't just sell cars - we deliver automotive excellence.

Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small performance shop to Botswana's premier destination for high-performance vehicles and custom modifications. Our team of certified technicians and automotive enthusiasts brings decades of combined experience to every project.

We carefully curate our inventory from the world's top manufacturers and work closely with each client to create their dream vehicle.
