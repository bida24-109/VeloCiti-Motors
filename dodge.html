Dodge

Domestic. Not Domesticated.

Dodge is known for pushing the boundaries of American muscle. Its lineup offers a bold combination of outrageous horsepower, aggressive styling, and thrilling performance both on the street and the drag strip.

Featured Vehicles

Dodge Charger SRT Hellcat

Four-Door Fury
The Charger SRT Hellcat is the world’s most powerful production sedan, boasting over 700 horsepower and an unmistakable roar. It’s a muscle car in a full-size body—perfect for speed-hungry families.

Dodge Challenger SRT Demon

Born to Dominate the Drag Strip
The SRT Demon is a street-legal drag monster with a supercharged 6.2L HEMI producing up to 840 horsepower. It’s engineered for quarter-mile domination, featuring launch assist, drag radials, and the world’s fastest 0–60 mph time.

Dodge Viper ACR

Track Weapon
The Viper ACR is a raw, race-bred supercar with a massive V10 engine and extreme aero package. It holds multiple track records and is revered for its unfiltered driving experience—this is Dodge at its most daring.
